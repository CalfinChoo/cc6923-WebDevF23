Welcome to Sudokumon!

An online trivia/puzzle game that blends the classic Sudoku gameplay with your Pokémon expertise!

Instructions

  • The goal of the game is to fill in all grid boxes with the correct Pokémon that fulfills the criteria of the corresponding column and row categories!
  • A wrong guess will cost a life so choose wisely! You lose when you run out of lives!
  • There will be at least one correct answer per box.
  • Once you select a Pokémon, you can't switch your answer or reuse that Pokémon.
  • When a special category lines up with a type, the Pokémon does NOT only have be that one type (unless that special category is "Monotype"). The Pokémon can have two types, as long as one of those types matches up with the type in the appropriate row.
