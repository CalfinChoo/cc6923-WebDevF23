Welcome to Sudokumon!

An online trivia/puzzle game that blends the classic Sudoku gameplay with your Pokémon expertise!

Instructions

  • The goal of the game is to fill in all grid boxes with the correct Pokémon that fulfills the criteria of the corresponding column and row categories!
  • A wrong guess will cost a life so choose wisely! You lose when you run out of lives!
  • There will be at least two correct answers per box.
  • Once you select a Pokémon, you can't switch your answer or reuse that Pokémon.
  • When the special column lines up with a type, the Pokémon does NOT only have be that one type. The Pokémon can have two types, as long as one of those types matches up with the type in the appropriate row.
  • When a specific region is a category, you must select a Pokémon that originated from that region.
  • Mega and GMAX Evolved Pokémon will be under the region where the normal form of the Pokémon originated.
